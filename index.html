Saipan's Must Try Restraunts

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks to dine when you are visiting Saipan

My top plaes to Dine

  1. Shirliy's Coffee Shop
  2. Gentle Brook Restraunt

Gentle Brook Restraunt | Susupe, Saipan

Gentle Brook Cafe serves a wide range of local cuisines. With my personal favorite being their curry dish. The offer a variety drinks as well and karaoke rooms.

What I love

  • The atmosphere
  • The staff
  • The location
